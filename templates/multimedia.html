Kala

Kerala Arts & Techno Learning Arena (KALA) is initiated by Kerala State Rutronix that offers learning programs in Diploma, D.Ed., Multimedia, CAD and SMAC-Technologies. It aims to positively impact the quality of one’s employability credentials through knowledge, skills and attitude. This multimedia education employability skill development project strives at bridging up the increasing gap between supply and demand of skilled professionals in Animation and Multimedia streams by focusing on providing quality and excellence in education with technologically demanding reviews and periodic updates.
