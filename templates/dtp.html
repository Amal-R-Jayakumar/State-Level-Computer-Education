About the Course

This six-month course provides hands on experience in various DeskTop Publishing softwares such as Page Maker, Corel Draw, Photoshopand ISM. Desktop publishing is a document layout & creation system that produces typographic quality text, images, etc., and enhances visual communication by making it possible to efficiently produce printed and electronic (online or onscreen) documents, without the need of expertise and expensive equipment that was once required. Upon completing this program, students will be able to create personal and business publications following current professional and industry standards. Students will acquire critical thinking skills to independently design and create publications.
